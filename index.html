Highest Revenue Lever
01

The Academy Is Positioned As A School. The Cohort Makes It One.

The Substack describes itself as "a safe and non-judgmental space where you can start your witchcraft journey at your own pace" and "everything you need to know about spell casting and manifesting." A school positioning. But the school has no structured intake. It's a feed, not a syllabus. The practitioner who's read Manifest Like A Witch, bought a spell jar, subscribed to the Academy, and started practising on her own has no productised next room. The offer stack runs from £22-£80 store products straight to the gated Mentoring door, with a £222 Clarity Session as the only 1:1 surface and nothing structured in between.

Manifest Like A Witch Cohort. Eight weeks. 60 seats. Twice yearly on seasonal cadence (autumn equinox intake + spring equinox intake). Weekly Lilly-led group call. Scheduled office hours. Three pillars: intention and ritual foundations, spellwork in practice, candle magic and the moon cycle. Cohort grads route into 1:1 Mentoring as the structured next step.

Cost of Waiting

Roughly £60K-£120K per cohort cycle not collected. Two cycles per year on the seasonal cadence = £120K-£240K in cohort revenue missed each year the room doesn't exist. Plus warm-list attrition: Academy subscribers who don't see a structured next door tend to renew once or twice and drift.

Six months from now: two full cohort cycles have come and gone. The competitor witchcraft teachers who do run cohort-shaped offers keep filling against your warm audience because they opened a door you haven't.

Fix: Manifest Like A Witch Cohort, £555-£1,500 seat (tested against warm-list quote response). Year-1 floor: £120K-£240K direct, two cohorts per year.
Foundational Capture Layer
02

1.2M Followers. No Segmentation Event Between Bio Tap And Aggregator.

Every follower who taps the bio lands on planoly.store - Patreon, Amazon, podcast, the store, and social all weighted equally. There's no quiz, no scorecard, no segmentation event. The practitioner who's ready for a £555-£1,500 cohort seat is in the same un-routed bucket as a follower who watched one reel and has never bought anything. Industry standard pull rate for a scorecard quiz against a warm IG audience this size is 3-5% inside 90 days. Against 1.2M followers, that's tens of thousands of incremental, segmented email subscribers - currently not happening.

"Which Witch Are You?" archetype quiz. Ten questions, two minutes. Returns a stage read (witch-curious, solo practitioner, cohort-ready, 1:1-ready) plus a Moon / Hearth / Sea / Garden Witch archetype overlay. Captures email. Segments by readiness. Tier-2 and Tier-3 results route to cohort waitlist. Tier-4 routes to Mentoring enquiry. Quiz lives at mysticprimrose.com/quiz and replaces planoly.store in the bio.

Cost of Waiting

Roughly £4K-£9K per month in nurture-driven cohort and Mentoring revenue not happening from un-segmented warm traffic. Compounds to £50K-£110K per year in incremental revenue from a list you don't yet have.

Six months from now: the email list is six months smaller than it should be when the first cohort opens. Every seat costs more to fill via paid acquisition instead of the warm-list engine.

Fix: "Which Witch Are You?" scorecard quiz. Segmented warm list. Year-1 floor: £50K-£110K from the list, compounds for life.
Hub + Routing Fix
03

The Bio Link Is An Aggregator. Aggregators Distribute Clicks. Hubs Direct Them.

planoly.store treats Patreon, podcast, Amazon, the store, and the Academy as equal-weight options. That works for content-only audiences. It's wrong for a brand that has £22-£950 paid products, a 600+ member Academy subscription, a £222 Clarity Session, and gated Mentoring. The aggregator hides the funnel under the noise. The highest-intent fraction - the practitioner ready to spend £500+ on a structured room - picks the option that looks most likely to lead somewhere and lands on a store homepage built for physical products, not cohort enrolment.

Two-door hub at mysticprimrose.com/with-lilly. Two primary doors above the fold: the cohort waitlist for the practitioner ready to do the work in a room, and the Mentoring enquiry for the witch ready for 1:1 work. Store, book, Academy, podcast, Patreon, and Amazon stay in a secondary row - accessible for the lower-intent traffic, secondary to the two funnel doors.

Cost of Waiting

Roughly £3K-£6K per month in cohort and 1:1 enquiries not happening from the highest-intent bio taps. £35K-£75K per year in revenue left in the aggregator. Plus the framing shift: an aggregator-shaped bio link signals creator brand. A hub-shaped bio link signals operator brand.

Six months from now: six more months of your highest-intent followers routing through a menu instead of a door.

Fix: Two-door hub. Cohort waitlist + Mentoring enquiry as primary surfaces. Year-1 floor: £35K-£75K from the routing fix alone.
Closed Loop · Book + Academy Wiring
04

*Manifest Like A Witch* Is The Highest-Trust Artefact In The Funnel. It Ends At The Last Page.

A reader who finishes a 200-page book on candle magic and spellwork has demonstrated more buying intent than 99% of the IG audience. Today that reader has nowhere structured to go next. No back-matter QR, no book-buyer email list segmentation, no "if you finished the book and want to do the practice in a room" callout. The book converts to spell-jar purchases and Academy subscriptions - but not to high-LTV cohort enrolment, because the wiring isn't pre-built.

Three small wiring moves: back-matter QR in the book pointing to the cohort waitlist; one Academy newsletter per quarter dedicated to the next cohort intake; cohort grads get a permanent Academy comp and a private alumni channel. The cohort feeds Academy retention. The Academy feeds cohort intake. Closed loop.

Cost of Waiting

Without the wiring, cohort 01 launches cold against the IG. With the wiring, it launches warm against the book buyers and Academy. The difference is roughly a 2x-3x fill rate on cohort 01. Year-1 floor: £40K-£85K isolated, with significant compound effect on all three layers above.

Six months from now: every book sold and every Academy subscription that could have been a cohort waitlist signup has left without the door being opened.

Fix: Back-matter QR + Academy intake newsletter + alumni channel. Year-1 floor: £40K-£85K isolated. Compounds with cohort fill rate.
