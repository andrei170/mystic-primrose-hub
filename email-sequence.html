Email 03 - The Quiz and the Warm List (Day 4)

Subject: The 1.2M followers who leave without a door

Hi Lilly,

Every IG follower who taps the bio lands on planoly.store. Patreon, Amazon, mysticprimrose.com, podcast, social - all weighted equally. There's no segmentation event. The practitioner ready to spend £555-£1,500 on a structured room lands in the same un-routed bucket as someone who's been following for a week and hasn't bought anything yet.

The fix is a scorecard quiz. "Which Witch Are You?" - ten questions, two minutes. Returns a stage read: witch-curious, solo practitioner, cohort-ready, or ready for 1:1 Mentoring. Plus a soft archetype overlay - Moon Witch, Hearth Witch, Sea Witch, Garden Witch - that's shareable enough to travel through the audience organically.

Captures email. Segments by readiness. Tier-2 and Tier-3 results route to the cohort waitlist. Tier-4 routes to Mentoring enquiry. Tier-1 stays in the Academy nurture for 60 days then re-quizzes.

Industry standard pull rate for a quiz against a warm audience this size is 3-5% inside 90 days. Against 1.2M followers, the conservative end is tens of thousands of incremental, segmented email subscribers. Year-1 floor from the list: £50K-£110K, compounds for life.

The quiz doesn't replace the Academy or the store. It routes the traffic that's currently leaving without a tracked door.

Email 04 - The Book Wiring (Day 6)

Subject: Manifest Like A Witch is half a funnel

Hi Lilly,

A reader who finishes a 200-page book on candle magic and spellwork has demonstrated more buying intent than 99% of the IG audience. That reader has nowhere structured to go next.

No back-matter QR. No book-buyer email list segmentation. No "if you finished the book and want to do the practice in a room" callout. The book converts to spell-jar purchases and Academy subscriptions - but not to cohort enrolment, because the cohort doesn't exist yet and the wiring isn't pre-built for when it does.

Three small moves, shipped alongside cohort 01:

  1. Back-matter QR in the book pointing to the cohort waitlist. Reprints carry it natively. Existing readers get the callout via a dedicated Academy newsletter.
  2. One Academy newsletter per quarter: "the next cohort opens [date]." Plus a permanent cohort waitlist link in the Academy welcome email.
  3. Cohort grads get a permanent Academy comp and a private alumni channel. The cohort feeds long-tail Academy retention. Closed loop.

This is the difference between cohort 01 launching warm (against the book buyers + Academy) and launching cold (against the general IG). Warm fill rate is roughly 2x-3x the cold launch. The wiring is the difference between the cohort selling out on the waitlist or needing a paid traffic campaign to fill.
